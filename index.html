I grew up building elaborate Lego sets from my own imagination. I loved being able to create planes, boats, or anything else I could come up with.
When I discovered my love for programming that feeling of creativity and joy was very reminiscent of when I was a kid. The ability to then create applications from my own ideas was exhilarating. Ever since then, I dove into any opportunity I could to learn and build projects, whether in groups or individually.
